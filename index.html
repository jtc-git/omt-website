One More Thing is an instrumental fusion group based out of the city of Toronto. Born by way of the University of Toronto, this group of musicians have performed as a unit under a variety of circumstances from jazz to pop to rock to musicals and over the years have forged a cohesive, unique and versitile identity culminating in their current sound. Set on creating original instrumental music with strong melodies and large improvisational elements, One More Thing draws on a large array of musical influences melding into something very much their own.
